Privacy Policy

​1. Quick Intro

This Privacy Policy tells you how we collect, use, share, and protect info from everyone who uses our marketplace – buyers, sellers, and just visitors.

2. What Info We Grab

We might collect:

From Buyers:

Your name, email, where to ship stuff, and payment info (handled by other companies).

What you bought and what you looked at on the site.

Any messages you sent to sellers or our support team.

From Sellers:

Your business name, how to reach you, and how you want to get paid (like your bank or PayPal).

What you're selling, your orders, and some numbers about how your stuff is doing.

Maybe some ID to prove who you are.

From Everyone:

Info about your device, your IP address, what kind of browser you're using, and what you do on our site.

Any messages you send through our forms or messaging system.

3. How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

Make and manage your buyer or seller account.

Help you list stuff, place orders, and make deals.

Send you updates about orders or other important stuff.

Process payments safely through other payment companies.

Help you if you need support.

Stop bad guys and keep the site safe.

See how the site is doing and make it better.

4. Services We Use

We might share info with companies we trust to help us do stuff:

Payment companies (like Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal).

Shipping companies (so you can track your stuff).

Email services (to send you emails about orders and deals).

Tools that show us how the site is doing.

ID check services (when sellers sign up).

These services only get the info they need and have to keep it safe.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

Keep you logged in.

Remember what's in your cart.

See how you use the site to help us make it better.

Help you find what you're looking for.

You can turn off cookies in your browser, but the site might not work as well.

6. Buyer &amp; Seller Info Sharing

Sellers and Buyers will see basic info, like names, addresses, and order info for the deals. Sellers can only use your information to facilitate fulfilling your order.

7. How Long We Keep Your Info

We only keep your info as long as we have to, to follow the law and do what we said we would. You can ask us to delete your account and your info, but we might have to keep some of it.

8. What You Can Do

You can:

See your personal info.

Change or update your info.

Ask us to delete your account.

Stop getting promotional emails.

Contact us to do these things.

9. How We Protect Your Info

We do our best to protect your info, but no system is perfect. Use a strong password and keep it safe.

10. Kids' Privacy

Our site is for people 13 and up. We don't collect info from kids on purpose.

11. Policy Changes

We might change this Privacy Policy sometimes. We'll post changes here with the date they go live.
